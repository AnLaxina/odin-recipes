Perfect Basic Burger Recipe

The easiest burger to make

A basic burger that anyone can make in no time!

This recipe uses only 5 ingredients and requires 15 minutes of cooking time.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Whisk together egg, pepper, and salt in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
  4. Form into 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
