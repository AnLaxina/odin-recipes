Deviled Eggs Recipe

Delicious for Easter brunch

A popular dish during Easter.

Adjust the amount of salt and sugar to suit your tastes. Adding onion and celery can enhance the flavor!

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped celery
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and peel hard-cooked eggs.
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yokes; set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl.
  4. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, onion, and celery; mix well and season with salt to taste.
  5. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  6. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.
